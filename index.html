ABOUT ME

JANINE SANTOS

What I think about Myself? The more I ask about this question into myself, the more I got lost into my own mind. I also confuse where should I start the story. It is definitely a simple question with a complex answer but I will start. I am Janine Santos 20 years old, I was born and raised in Laguna. I am a BSIT student at Mindoro State University. I currently live in Calapan City, Oriental Mindoro. Honestly, I don't want to talk about myself because for me I am very emotional woman. I'm the type that gets hurt easily and gets tired quickly because of life's challenges, but nothing in this life will be easy, we will cry, laugh, scream and be silent. What you do to get over that wall is what defines you. There's more than one way over a wall! Remember that! We learn after our hard times what it means to love ourselves wholeheartedly and how important it is to look in the mirror and say to yourself, " You are beautiful." When I'm at a point in my life where I'm having a hard time facing the challenge, I just become positive, and I'm the same with my friends and family. For me, thinking positive thoughts enhances self-love and confidence.But now my only goal in life is to finish my studies even though the challenges are very difficult. To find a job that matches my course or even not.
